Our mission is to inspire students in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) through hands-on robotics experience while developing leadership, teamwork, and professional skills.

## Safety Guidelines {#safety-guidelines}

Safety is our top priority. All members must:

1. **Complete safety training** before using any tools or equipment
2. **Wear appropriate PPE** (safety glasses, closed-toe shoes)
3. **Follow tool-specific safety procedures**
4. **Report all injuries** to a mentor immediately
5. **Keep workspace clean** and organized
6. **Never work alone** in the shop

## Code of Conduct {#code-of-conduct}

All team members are expected to:

### Respect
- Treat all team members, mentors, and competitors with respect
- Value diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- Listen actively and communicate constructively

### Responsibility
- Complete assigned tasks on time
- Take ownership of mistakes and learn from them
- Help keep the workspace clean and organized

### Integrity
- Be honest in all communications
- Give credit where credit is due
- Follow competition rules and regulations

### Gracious Professionalism
- Help other teams when possible
- Compete hard but fairly
- Represent the team positively at all times

### Consequences
Violations of the code of conduct may result in:
1. Verbal warning
2. Written warning
3. Meeting with parents/guardians
4. Suspension from team activities
5. Removal from the team
