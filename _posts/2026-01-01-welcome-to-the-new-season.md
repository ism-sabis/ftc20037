We're excited to kick off the 2025-2026 robotics season! After months of preparation, our team is ready to take on this year's challenge.

## What's New This Season

This year brings several exciting changes to our team:

- **New Team Members:** We've welcomed several new students who are eager to learn
- **Upgraded Equipment:** Thanks to our sponsors, we have new tools and materials
- **Expanded Outreach:** We're planning more community events than ever

## Season Goals

Our goals for this season include:

1. Build a competitive robot that excels at the game objectives
2. Qualify for the state championship
3. Expand our STEM outreach to reach more students
4. Continue developing our documentation and engineering process
